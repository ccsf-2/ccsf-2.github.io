More about open source

Free, open source software is software that is shared freely and available to build upon. It is a fun way to apply your skills to advance projects and social causes. You can participate by writing or reviewing code, answering users' questions, translating the interface to another format, making video tutorials to help new users, and myriad other ways. Open source software is often produced by lots of people collaborating across various fields and mediums, and this event specially welcomes everyone to that style of collaboration.

Open source participation is one way to gain experience, skills and make connections that will last you through your career. Volunteer staff will include professionals and academics who use open source daily.
